## What is ScrumSurvivor?

ScrumSurvivor creates a photorealistic digital version of you for video meetings. It uses artificial intelligence to move your avatar’s lips in sync with your speech. This software works with Microsoft Teams and other video call platforms. It runs as a virtual camera, which means you can select it just like you would select a standard webcam.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

To run this software, your computer needs these features:

*   Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   Processor: An Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 or better.
*   Memory: At least 8GB of RAM.
*   Graphics Card: A dedicated NVIDIA graphics card with at least 4GB of video memory. This is necessary for the AI to process images in real time.
*   Camera: A standard USB or built-in webcam.

## ⚙️ Initial Setup

When you open the application for the first time, you must configure your avatar.

1.  Locate the ScrumSurvivor icon on your desktop or in your Start menu.
2.  Open the program.
3.  You will see a prompt to choose a source image. This photo should show your face clearly with a neutral expression.
4.  The application will process your photo. This may take a few seconds.
5.  Select your physical webcam from the dropdown menu in the settings tab.
6.  Click the Test button to see a preview of the avatar.

## 📹 Using in Meetings

The application creates a virtual camera. You use this to pipe your avatar into your meetings.

1.  Open Microsoft Teams, Zoom, or your preferred meeting software.
2.  Go to the Video settings menu.
3.  Find the Camera selection dropdown.
4.  Select "ScrumSurvivor" from the list of available cameras.
5.  Check your preview to make sure your avatar appears.
6.  Join your meeting as you usually would. The software will detect your microphone input and sync the avatar motion automatically.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Problems

If you run into issues, check these frequent solutions.

*   **The avatar does not move:** Ensure your microphone is connected and not muted. Check that the application has permission to access your microphone in your Windows Privacy settings.
*   **The image is frozen:** Close the meeting application and the ScrumSurvivor app. Open ScrumSurvivor first, then open your meeting software.
*   **The video is slow or choppy:** Close other programs that use your graphics card. High usage from other software can slow down the AI processing.
*   **The camera is not listed:** Restart your computer to refresh the virtual camera drivers.
*   **Error during startup:** Ensure your display drivers are up to date. Visit the website of your graphics card manufacturer to download the latest driver software.

## 📏 Best Practices for Quality

For the best experience, follow these tips.

*   Use a high-quality photo of your face for the avatar. Avoid photos with filters or heavy shadows.
*   Sit in a well-lit room. This helps the app track your movements better if you want to use live input modes.
*   Keep background noise low. The lip-sync AI works best when it can clearly distinguish your voice from static or background sounds.
*   Position your microphone near your mouth to ensure the software hears you clearly.

## 🔒 Privacy and Security

ScrumSurvivor processes your video feed locally on your computer. Your images and video data do not transmit to external servers. The software functions entirely offline after you complete the initial installation. This ensures that your meeting details remain private within your local machine.

## 💡 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this work with every meeting platform?**
Yes, it acts as a system-wide webcam, so it works with any software that allows you to choose a camera source.

**Can I switch between my real camera and the avatar?**
You can change the camera source in your meeting settings at any time during a call.

**Does this affect my internet speed?**
No, because the processing happens locally on your computer, your internet bandwidth usage remains the same as using a standard camera.

**Can I use multiple avatars?**
The current version supports one active avatar profile at a time. You can replace the source photo in the settings tab whenever you want to update your look.
